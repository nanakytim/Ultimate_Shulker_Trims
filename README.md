## ✨ Context
**This mod allows players to add armor trims to their shulker boxes, the trims will render on the icons and the blocks alike! Helpful to customise and differentiate your boxes.**
## 🔓 Safe to use
Your Shulker boxes are safe, this only adds rendering and armor trim NBT Data, so even if you decide to remove this mod, your Shulker boxes will be fine, and keep their dye and content intact!

![Six different trims applied to dyed shulker boxes](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/6345fefbc5ca5bb77d68cf9d693552354fef1b40.png)

## ⚙️ No configuration required, ready to use!

## 🔗 Compatibility
Clients with this mod can join vanilla servers, and servers with this mod can host vanilla clients. One jar supports both Fabric clients and servers.  
Compatible with other mods that change trim rendering should be supported.  
**This mod does not natively work with modded trims, but those can be added with a datapack and a resource pack!**
