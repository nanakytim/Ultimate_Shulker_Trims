## This mod allows players to add armor trims to their shulker boxes, the trims will render on the icons and the blocks alike! Helpful to customise and differentiate your boxes.
### Your Shulker boxes are safe, this only adds rendering and armor trim NBT Data, so even if you decide to remove this mod, your Shulker boxes will be fine, and keep their dye and content intact!
